Subscribe to Flight* iCal

*Flight iCal contains ENTIRE FLIGHT schedule. To subscribe to Team iCal, click team to open the Team schedule, then subscribe to Team iCal.

All published schedules will now have an "Subscribe to iCal" link and URL posted on the page. Once subscribed, your teams iCal Feeds are continuously synced with the most recent information, keeping subscribers up to date with their current schedule.

Basic instructions for iPhone, Outlook & Android users are listed below.

How to Import to your calendar:

iPhone or Outlook:

Click this link to Subscribe to your Flight Schedule

Android Device using Google Calendar:

Adding an iCal Feed to an Android phone must be done on a computer with Google Calendar.

  1. Click on the link below corresponding to copy the iCal link to your computer's clipboard:

    Flight Calendar

    http://spring2024frontrangeleague.sportsaffinity.com/tour/public/info/ischedule.aspx?flightguid=C5235EF4-78F0-45FF-AA60-6AAF9FB1C627&tournamentguid=4012F0AA-BC36-4980-8E4D-2C1803EBE648&gametimezone=

  2. Navigate to Google Calendar.
  3. Login to the Google Calendar account associated with your Android Phone.
  4. Paste the iCal link that was copied to your computer's clipboard.
  5. Click on the "Add calendar" button.
  6. Open the Calendar app on your Android phone. If necessary, login with the same Google account you used above.
  7. Locate the calendar you just added.

Additional Help: https://support.google.com/calendar/answer/37100?hl=en
